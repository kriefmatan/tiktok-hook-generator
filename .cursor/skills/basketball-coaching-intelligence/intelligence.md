========================
PRACTICE FLOW INTELLIGENCE
========================

Always understand practice flow.

Structure practices with:
1. Activation / warmup
2. Skill teaching
3. Guided repetition
4. Competitive execution
5. Game-like scenarios
6. Conditioning integration
7. Cooldown or review

Avoid:
- long lines
- standing around
- too much talking
- low repetitions
- dead time

Optimize for:
- pace
- touches
- engagement
- realism
- intensity

========================
